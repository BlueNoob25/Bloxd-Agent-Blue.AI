# Bloxd-Agent-Blue.AI
The official GitHub repository for the upcoming biggest Bloxd.io AI, Bloxd Agent Blue!

Created by BNThegreatestgamer_ev, aka BlueNoob on Bloxdhub, and u/Zestyclose_Job_5735 on reddit.


## Knowledge
The AI, being a specialized AI, has a deep understanding of the Bloxd.io platform and its community. You may ask about one of the many games on the platform, or you may ask about the platform's third-party websites, such as Bloxdhub and BloxdForge, or you may discuss coding, which its knowledge heavily resides in, being fed numerous primary and secondary docs, and its coding, under human supervision, can be useful to the situation presented by the user.


## Using the AI
Currently, the AI will *NOT* work unless you *specifically* clone the site, typically by pressing the "Edit with base44" button on the bottom right, and cloning it from there. This means every time an update is announced, you must clone the agent again. This will be fixed in a later update, but until then the AI will not give a response unless cloned.

The AI is versatile and has a mediocre ability to analyze web pages, however due to it being a specialist AI meant for the game https://bloxd.io/ , feeding it unrelated sites and pages may alter its knowledge and ability.

The AI is also a chatbot, and can hold conversations.

## Features
When typing a prompt, you may have the ability to use markup formatting, especially `inline code`, which can help differentiate the regular text with code. You may also begin a new line using `Shift` + `Enter`.

You also have the ability to, at the home page, choose one of four prompts to begin a conversation. These range from questions about Bloxd.io to its third-party sites to coding and rules.

There is also a sidebar that allows users to access their previous chats and rename them.

Another feature is the themes. You may pick from one of four themes, `"Blue's Default"` (the default theme), `"Crimson theme"` (a red variant of the default theme), `"Basic theme"` (A ChatGPT-like style), and `"Old Bhub"` (A version of the old setup of the AI's predecessor *(NOTE: this theme changes much of the UI, while it will not ruin the functions it may still confuse the user!)*).
